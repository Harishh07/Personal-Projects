DESCRIPTION:
Music has been my passion since my childhood. Its has provided me the space to be creative and mainly to be myself.
From starting with a Yamaha PSR to tweaking plugins in DAW, Recording, Mixing and Mastering, I found the technical aspects of music production quite interesting and I tried to blend it well with my acoustic music knowledge. I'm currently working on my New Album set to be released soon. Stay Tuned!
Check out my works below and do follow me for more updates regarding my work!
Cheers!!
